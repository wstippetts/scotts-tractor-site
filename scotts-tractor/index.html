About Scott

Hi, I'm Scott.

I'm a local owner-operator based out of Fruitland, Idaho, and I take real pride in the work I put out. Whether it's a pasture that's gotten out of hand, a road that needs some attention, or a digging job — I show up with the right equipment and get it done.

Brush hogging is my bread and butter. There's something satisfying about turning overgrown land into something clean and usable again. My 2017 Mahindra 2540 is built for a full day's work, and I've got the attachments to handle whatever your property needs.

I'm owner-operated — meaning when you call, you get me, and when I show up, it's me doing the work.
